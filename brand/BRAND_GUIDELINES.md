# ARKTIK Brand Guidelines (Partner-Safe)

## Brand posture
ARKTIK is:
- family-first, student-protective, governance-forward
- evidence-first, standards-aligned, outcomes-focused
- rigorous without being adversarial
- disciplined in corrections/versioning and authenticity

## Naming
Use:
- ARKTIK Education
- ARKTIK Press
- ARKTIK News & Media
- ARKTIK Lifestyle

Do not invent new sub-brands or abbreviations that imply endorsement.

## Claims you may make (if accurate)
- “Textbook-powered operating system” (Textbook Engine)
- “Offline-first / audit-ready outputs” (where applicable)
- “Closed cohorts and parent permission for minors” (Publishing & Speaking program)
- “Corrections/versioning posture for published materials”

## Claims you may not make
- Accreditation claims unless ARKTIK has explicitly published them
- “Official partnership” with universities, districts, agencies, or brands unless in a signed agreement
- “Open-source” or “community-built” descriptions

## Safety language (recommended)
ARKTIK is family-first and operates a zero-tolerance posture for sexualized speech/content.
See: `../CHILD_PROTECTION_STANDARD.md`.

## Tone
- Clear, firm, non-inflammatory
- Do not demonize other platforms or institutions
- Emphasize “healthy alternative,” craft, belonging, and outcomes

## Attribution
When referencing ARKTIK materials, preserve attribution and link to canonical sources.
