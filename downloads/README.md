# Downloads (Public)

This folder is a **controlled distribution vault** for public-safe ARKTIK PDFs and one-pagers.

## What belongs here
- Parent-safe program overviews
- Partner-safe one-pagers (no internal ops)
- Press-safe summaries (no student data)
- Samples that demonstrate quality without exposing operational pipelines

## What does NOT belong here
- Any student/client PII
- Internal playbooks, workflows, automation designs
- Pricing sheets intended for private sales conversations
- Anything governed by NDA or a commercial agreement
